    What We Do

    The primary focus of our organization is to provide custom development and high quality technical resources to assist our clients achieve their goals with the help of our services.

    Whether we use your tools or we bring our extensive toolbox, our people will integrate with your environment and your team. We’ll learn your business and understand your goals. We then align our work to achieving your business goals as part of your team. That means, involving your staff and training them to take over when we’re done.

    Data Warehousing

    Metamor Systems started in a complex data warehousing environment integrating more than twenty data sources. From data discovery and ETL processes through dashboard roll-outs, we can help with any aspect or handle the whole project for you.     Data Management

    We cover the full data lifecycle of services: Data Discovery, Cleansing, ETL Processes, Master Data Management (MDM), Probabilistic Matching, Data Governmance and Defining KPI's. Today your data is likely housed in all kinds of locations including: custom applications, cloud services and vendor systems. IT Transformers' data experts analyze, harmonize and resolve issues with any of your data. This enables your organization to truly unlock the power and insights that are hiding in plain sight.     Internet of Things

    IOT provides a huge potential to improve everyone’s lives and automate actions based on device telemetry. However, the nascent stage of many IOT devices and applications are often problematic and frustrating for business and consumers. Along with our partners, we can create scalable and secure cloud-native IOT applications that perform well. Whether it’s mapping data schemas between different devices or creating strategies for redundancy and graceful failure/start-up, we go beyond the hype to help your IOT solution unlock its potential. More...
